# Changelog

All notable milestones for the EvoRadar engine and platform.

---

## 2026-03-26
**Engine live on evoradar.ai** — 1,650+ ideas evaluated. Daily generation running. Public showcase repository created.

## 2026-03-25
**Signal awareness expansion** — Broader global signal coverage. Engine now adjusts exploration aggressiveness dynamically per run.

## 2026-03-20
**Multi-flavor daily pipeline** — Engine now runs multiple generation approaches daily in a single automated pipeline.

## 2026-03-14
**Domain migration to evoradar.ai** — Dedicated domain with full auth and email infrastructure.

## 2026-03-09
**AI-native architecture redesign** — Fundamental shift from scripted pipeline to autonomous thinking. The engine reasons freely, then structures conclusions. Self-evolution: the engine improves based on outcomes, not manual tuning.

## 2026-03-01
**Reality check layer added** — Discovered that top-scoring ideas failed real-world review. Engine now actively verifies competitive landscape and checks for structural market barriers.

## 2026-02
**Scoring overhaul** — A single catastrophic weakness now kills the idea regardless of other strengths. This mirrors how real startups fail.

## 2026-01
**First scale output** — 2,000+ ideas generated. Kill rate evolved from 53% to 90% as the engine learned to be ruthless.

## 2025 (pre-public)
**Five complete rewrites** before the engine produced anything worth sharing. v1 generated 60 ideas and killed zero of them. Each version taught the engine what not to do.

---

*The engine evolves continuously. This changelog captures major milestones only.*
